Downtown Disney Resorts

The Downtown Disney Resort Area includes four resorts located around Downtown Disney in the Walt Disney World Resort. Two of these are Disney Vacation Club resorts.

The resorts include:

  • Disney's Port Orleans Resort-Riverside (Moderate Resort) (Formerly Disney's Dixie Landings)
  • Disney's Port Orleans Resort-French Quarter (Moderate Resort)
  • Disney's Old Key West Resort (Disney Vacation Club Villas available)
  • Disney's Saratoga Springs Resort & Spa (Disney Vacation Club Villas available)
