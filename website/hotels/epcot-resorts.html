EPCOT Resorts

The Epcot Resort Area is the area between Epcot and Disney's Hollywood Studios at the Walt Disney World Resort which consists of six individual resorts, five of which that are interconnected via footpaths and waterways surrounding a large central lake known as Crescent Lake.

The EPCOT Resorts include:

  • Disney's Yacht Club Resort (Deluxe Resort)
  • Disney's Beach Club Resort (Deluxe Resort) (Disney Vacation Club Villas available)
  • Disney's BoardWalk Inn (Deluxe Resort) (Disney Vacation Club Villas available)
  • Disney's Caribbean Beach Resort (Moderate Resort)
  • Walt Disney World Dolphin* (Deluxe Resort)
  • Walt Disney World Swan* (Deluxe Resort)

Note: The Walt Disney World Swan and Dolphin Resorts are operated by Starwood Hotels and Resorts Worldwide, but still maintain certain perks such as park transportation, Extra Magic Hours, and length-of-stay park tickets. Swan and Dolphin guests are NOT provided with Disney's Magical Express transportation.
