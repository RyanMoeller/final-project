Disney Transportation

Disney Transport is the privately operated transportation system at the Walt Disney World Resort in Florida. Most movements are made using buses that run along the resort's public roads maintained by the Reedy Creek Improvement District and private roads. Other transportation is provided by the Walt Disney World Monorail System, watercraft, and parking lot trams.

Transportation is provided on Disney property to all park and resort guests at no additional charge. Different modes of transport are available depending at what Disney resort you are staying at, assuming you are staying in one of the Disney owned hotels.

Buses are the primary transport for most hotels to the parks. However, some hotels have access to the Friendship Boat system to travel from your resort to the parks. Other hotels have access to the monorail system to reach the parks from their hotel. If you're really lucky, a small handful of hotels have walkways from the hotel to certain parks.

Disney's Contemporary Resort is a very short walk away from Magic Kingdom. The Yatch and Beach Club and Disney's Boardwalk Inn have walkways that lead to both EPCOT and Disney's Hollywood Studios.
