Extra Magic Hours

Extra Magic Hours offer a rare and unique opportunity to enjoy additional time in Walt Disney World parks—above and beyond the standard operating schedule—at no extra charge. This special perk is offered to Guests of select Walt Disney World Resort hotels.

Each day, one of the 4 Walt Disney World theme parks opens one hour early or remains open up to 2 hours after regular closing time. Additionally, Disney Water Parks offer Extra Magic Hours during select times of year.

How It Works:

To take advantage of extended park hours, simply visit the designated park at the designated time. Upon check-in at a Walt Disney World Resort hotel, Guests will receive a schedule detailing when each park offers Extra Magic Hours, so you can be sure to plan your stay accordingly.

In addition to the schedule, the following guidelines will help you make the most of Extra Magic Hours:

  • Morning Hours: Arrive up to an hour before the designated park is scheduled to open and show your valid park ticket and Resort ID at the park turnstiles.
  • Evening Hours: A valid theme park ticket and Resort ID are required. Each member of your party will need to present a Resort ID to access evening Extra Magic Hours offerings.
