Fantasyland

Fantasyland is themed in a medieval-faire/carnival style, in the words of Walt Disney: "Fantasyland is dedicated to the young at heart and to those who believe that when you wish upon a star, your dreams come true." Classic attractions include It's a Small World, Peter Pan's Flight, The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh, Mickey's PhilharMagic, Prince Charming Regal Carrousel, and Mad Tea Party. Fantasyland was recently expanded to nearly double its size and new attractions and other guest offerings were added. The last attraction to be opened is the Seven Dwarfs Mine Train. The ride is set in the mines that the dwarfs from Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs work in. It features a new ride system which simulates the swaying and tipping one would expect to experience in a mine cart. This opened May 28, 2014.

Tips:

For the most part, Fantasyland attractions have relatively short wait times so they're fun to do to kill time between FastPass returns or while waiting for dinner reservations. One exception is Peter Pan's Flight, which has gained a surprising amount of popularity from guests in recent years. This makes it a definite FastPass selection since wait times are usually very long. The queue has also recently implemented interactive options so, similar to Space Mountain wait times could see a slight increase.

Also, the newest addition to Fantasyland is the Seven Dwarfs Mine Train but I have not visited Disney World since this opened so I can't offer any specific info about this attraction, but since its new I would imagine that wait times are fairly high so try to get a FastPass for this one.
